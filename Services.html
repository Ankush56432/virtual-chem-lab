Drug Discovery in Bioinformatics

Master the integration of databases, docking tools, and screening platforms for in silico drug discovery.

Topics Covered
  • Drug Databases: UniProt, DrugBank, PubChem, ChEMBL, BindingDB
  • Protein & Ligand Preparation: RCSB PDB, AlphaFold, SWISS-MODEL

  • Docking Tools: AutoDock Vina, SwissDock
  • Post-Docking Analysis: LigPlot+, Discovery Studio
  • Virtual Screening: SwissADME, ADMETSAR
