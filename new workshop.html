Advanced Molecular Dynamics Simulation Workshop

Master the art of molecular dynamics simulations using GROMACS and analyze protein behavior, drug-protein interactions, and biomolecular systems.

About the Workshop
Dive deep into the world of Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations with this comprehensive hands-on workshop. Learn to simulate biomolecular systems, analyze protein dynamics, study drug-protein interactions, and understand the behavior of complex molecular systems over time. This workshop is designed for researchers, students, and professionals who want to master GROMACS, one of the most powerful MD simulation packages used in computational chemistry and biophysics. From system preparation to advanced trajectory analysis, you'll gain practical skills to conduct research-grade MD simulations.
